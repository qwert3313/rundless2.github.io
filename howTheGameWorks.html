How the Game Works

How to get Discount on Products in the Shop

  • Download the game.
  • You will receive a discount code every time you reach 60 points, no matter in what level you are
  • The value of the discount code depends on the level you reached the 60 points in.
  • Go in the shop to the section 'Enter Code'
  • Enter your Code
  • Chose the product you want to get discount on
  • Proceed the checkout

Warning: Once you enter the code, it cannot be used again.

What Are Season T-Shirts?

Every 4 months, a new season starts, bringing a newly designed T-Shirt with it. You can earn the current season's T-Shirt by reaching 100 points in level 4. You can only get the season T-Shirt from the current season.

How to Get Season T-Shirts

  • Once you reach 100 points in Level 4, you receive a code.
  • Enter the code in the 'Enter Code' section of the shop.
  • Proceed the checkout

Warning: Once you enter the code, it cannot be used again.
